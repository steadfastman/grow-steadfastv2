A Blueprint for Biblical Manhood

We offer different levels of engagement to meet men where they are, from intensive one-on-one coaching to a supportive community. Each path is designed to equip you with the tools and accountability you need to stand firm and live with purpose.

The Transformation Blueprint

For the Man Ready for Deep, Lasting Change

Our 1-on-1 coaching is a deep dive into building your life on a biblical foundation. Over 3 or 6 months, we'll work together to create a personalized action plan for your faith, family, and work. This includes bi-weekly sessions, unlimited email/text support, and curated resources. The outcome is a comprehensive life transformation rooted in biblical truth.

The Forge

Iron Sharpens Iron

The Forge is an 8-week group cohort that brings men together to tackle a specific area of life, such as marriage, leadership, or purpose. Through the power of brotherhood, shared accountability, and biblical teaching, you'll gain insights and encouragement alongside other men on the same journey. As Proverbs 27:17 says, "As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another."

The Steadfast Alliance

Consistent Encouragement for the Journey

The journey of a steadfast man is a marathon, not a sprint. The Steadfast Alliance is a low-cost monthly membership providing the consistent encouragement you need. Benefits include weekly devotionals, a private community forum for support and discussion, and a monthly live Q&A call with Coach Nick to keep you anchored and moving forward.
